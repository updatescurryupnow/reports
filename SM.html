#2 focus — Avg per guest (dine-in) across periods the biggest sales lever

After prime, this is the number to move. Avg per guest is dine-in spend per cover. SM's AM goal is $22 and PM is $28. Lifting PM spend per guest flows straight to sales, which pulls prime down.
AM avg/guestPM avg/guestat goalbelow goal
How SM gets prime under budget:
  1. Hold true labor at 28%. Labor swung from a tight 24.5% (P5) to 31-34% (P6-P7). OT is the main driver — SM ran 99-108 OT hrs per period. Zero-OT scheduling is the biggest lever.
  2. Keep ordering at 20% to control COGS. Ordering ran 23-26%; actual COGS hit 33% in P4. If we don't order more, we can't use more — ordering discipline is how COGS stays on target.
  3. Grow the evening. PM avg/guest and PM dine-in are the sales levers — lifting them dilutes fixed cost and pulls prime down.
