#2 focus — Avg per guest (dine-in) across periods the biggest sales lever

After prime, this is the number to move. Avg per guest is dine-in spend per cover. AM sits near its $25 goal; PM misses $35 nearly every period. Lifting PM spend per guest flows straight to sales, which pulls prime down.
AM avg/guestPM avg/guestat goalbelow goal
How the store gets prime under budget:
  1. Cut the ordering line. Ordering ran 23-29% against a 20% budget every period. Fastest lever — Sysco to 2 orders/week, Modesto to 3, sized to forecast.
  2. Kill overtime. OT hit 141.7 hours in P6 and drives labor toward 40%. Zero-OT scheduling is the biggest labor recovery.
  3. Grow sales to dilute fixed cost. Every shift metric — PM avg/guest, dine-in %, beverage attach — lifts sales, which pulls prime % down.
