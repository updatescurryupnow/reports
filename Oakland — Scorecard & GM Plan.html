  • Modesto and SJ Distributors can both deliver multiple times a week, so small frequent orders from them are good budget control. Order only what is needed each drop; no need to consolidate or stock up on these two.

Renzo's 4-week plan to get the budget under control

Operationally: zero meal violations, 3PD back under 1%, labor floored to the minimum safe crew. Strategically: a clear AK decision on the unit.
Week 1
Set a minimum-viable labor floor
Schedule the smallest safe crew on the slow dayparts so fixed labor stops eating the store. Hold OT at zero (already compliant) and build breaks into the schedule to drive meal violations to zero.
Week 2
Fix the 3PD regression
Install the order-accuracy SOP (KDS-done when correct, name on box, check on receipt) and an expo checklist. Train an out-of-stock substitution script so 86ed items are handled cleanly and stop generating negatives and error charges.
Week 3
Protect margin on low volume
Tighten ordering hard to forecasted sales with a mid-week check; on a low-volume store, over-ordering is costly. Target food/COGS under 24%.
Week 4
Strategic review
Package the four-week numbers for the AK viability conversation: prime, labor, traffic trend, and what a realistic floor looks like. Confirm zero meal violations and 3PD back under 1%.
